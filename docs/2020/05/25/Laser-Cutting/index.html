Laser Cutting

Last Updated on 2020 Jun 19 17:08:13

Ill fated laser cutting……

Setup

My intention was to design “something that can be inserted into each other”.

However the basic design pattens are almost the same - it’s always some squares or circles, with slots…. So I made a very innovative attempt - Make it a triangle! Add a hypotenuse (in Chinese: 斜边)!!!!

So here is my design:

In the sketch file it consists of 2 triangles close to each other (to save material). I want to print 10~20 pieces out to make something.

Hard experience

During the lecture the laser cutter was broken. Who thought it’s just a nightmare-like beginning……

I came to Fablab on May.24. Very unfortunately the cutter can not receive any files from the computer.

I came again on May.25 - This time we’re finally able to use it; but it cannot recognize my output files!!!

I made several attempts:

  • Use different output formats: FAILED
  • Use AutoCAD on that computer to open my output file: FAILED
    • The AutoCAD was crashed!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
  • Use AutoCAD on that computer to open my output file: FAILED
    • The LATEST VERSION!!!!! of AutoCAD on my computer was also CRASHED!!!!!

Um…. I don’t know what to do now :(

A Turn For The Better

After asking Songhui Cao for help, he told me that I should not use the “export” tool, but “Save As”. I tried - And it’s a success!

Also, after debugging and repairing, the laser cutter is finally repaired. And I found that if you want the cutter to receive files correctly, you should not attach any other USB device to the PC except for the cutter. If unfortunately you did so, you’ll have to restart the cutter.

Finally I got it to move and printed those pieces out!

The parameters has to be fine tuned - Since it’s 4mm thick wood, you’ll have to cut with 92% power, 3% speed twice. And when trying the parameters, one of those pieces was a little broken (I circled it out with red line.)

I used the last 11 pieces to combine a meaningless but coooooool stuff (my roommate says it reminds him of the film: V for Vendetta)

Not bad!
